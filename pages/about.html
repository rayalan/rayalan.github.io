About

Hi!

I’m Alan, and I muse about software engineering, especially the systems and processes that deliver anticipated software in a timely and economical manner. Or at least, that’s the goal. Most of us know what happens when those processes aren’t working – software starts shipping late, or with bugs, or at great expense – or all of the above. In the nightmare scenario, it doesn’t ship at all1.

In other words, this is the world I live in:
